Publisher’s Abstract

As the translator says, this book is indeed “a literary and spiritual jewel.” Though there are truths not touched upon by the author, we are given a refreshing glimpse of a life that can truly be said to be without guile. We appreciate the beauty of the light shining through Paula’s life, and the saving, transforming power of God depicted in this story.
