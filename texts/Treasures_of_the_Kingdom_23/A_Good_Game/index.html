A Good Game

Have you ever played “What Time is it, Mr. Clock?” Here is an outdoor game that works the same way.

One person will be the “Bible” and stand facing away from the rest. The rest should stand in a row on the other side of the yard, taking steps toward the “Bible” during the game.

To start, players call out “What book is in the Bible?” The “Bible” should name a book, like “First Peter!” If the book is in the Old Testament, everyone takes 1 step. If it is in the New Testament, they take 2 steps.

The game continues until the “Bible” calls out “Revelation!” and turns around to try to tag someone. Everyone must run back to the starting line or get caught!
