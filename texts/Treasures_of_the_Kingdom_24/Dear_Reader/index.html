Dear Reader

As we prepare this issue for publication, we pray that God is preparing the hearts of those who receive it. “I have planted, Apollos watered; but God gave the increase.”* (I Corinthians 3:6) We can do the work God appoints merely as unworthy servants.

Looking back over the past four years of publishing this paper, we can see how God has directed and led. The future is just as unknown as when we first started, but we have the priviledge of walking with One who knows the way. Your prayers are greatly appreciated.

We would be glad to hear from any of you. We welcome questions and would be very happy to hear how the Lord has been helping you.

We are a God-fearing family that includes Rick and Krista Erickson, Laura (22), Skye (21), Joel (20), Kara (18), and Amanda (10). The publishing of Treasures of the Kingdom is mainly done by Laura and Joel, with help (guidance, proofreading, and contributions) from others. We appreciate your prayers, for we certainly can’t do this without God’s help!

In the King’s service,
The Editors
