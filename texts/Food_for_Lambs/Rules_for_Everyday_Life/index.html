Rules for Everyday Life

“Let your speech be alway with grace, seasoned with salt.”* (Colossians 4:6)

“Withhold not good from them to whom it is due, when it is in the power of thine hand to do it.”* (Proverbs 3:27)

“Walk in wisdom toward them that are without.”* (Colossians 4:5)

“Do all things without murmurings and disputings.”* (Philippians 2:14)

“Let another man praise thee, and not thine own mouth.”* (Proverbs 27:2)

“Be thou diligent to know the state of thy flocks.”* (Proverbs 27:23)

“Eat so much as is sufficient for thee.”* (Proverbs 25:16)

“Be not wise in your own conceits.”* (Romans 12:16)

“Abstain from all appearance of evil.”* (I Thessalonians 5:22)

“See that none render evil for evil unto any man.”* (I Thessalonians 5:15)

“Be kindly affectioned one to another with brotherly love.”* (Romans 12:10)

“Be not overcome of evil, but overcome evil with good.”* (Romans 12:21)

“Be content with such things as ye have.”* (Hebrews 13:5)

“Whatsoever thy hand findeth to do, do it with thy might.”* (Ecclesiastes 9:10)

“Let all things be done with charity.”* (I Corinthians 16:14)

“Count it all joy when ye fall into divers temptations.”* (James 1:2)

“Keep thyself pure.”* (I Timothy 5:22)

“In everything give thanks.”* (I Thessalonians 5:18)

“Keep yourselves in the love of God.”* (Jude 1:21)

“Praying always with all prayer and supplication in the Spirit, and watching thereunto with all perseverance and supplication for all saints.”* (Ephesians 6:18)
