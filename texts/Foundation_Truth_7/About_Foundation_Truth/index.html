About Foundation Truth

We publish Foundation Truth by faith, its only support being free-will offerings that God lays on the hearts of His children to keep this ministry supplied. Therefore, there is no subscription price.

If the Lord lays on your heart to contribute, please make out any checks or money orders to Richard Erickson or Abigail Spinks. We have difficulty depositing any monies made out to Foundation Truth or any other past or present publication name.

Address subscription requests or correspondence to:

Foundation Truth
P.O. Box 1212
Jefferson, OR 97352
email

Elsewhere in this issue you will see that Abigail is retiring from editing the “Dear Princess” section. Please send submissions for “Tell What He’s Done for You” to the post office or email address above, and pray for us.

We continue to experiment with organization of the magazine; hence, some age groups have a section or articles addressed to them specifically, while other articles are of general interest. Some columns will be regular, and others occasional. The absence of names on articles is not meant to frustrate, but to keep the focus on the message, rather than the messenger. Please pray for us, that the Lord’s will be accomplished in both format and content.

All scripture quotations are from the King James Version unless otherwise indicated.
