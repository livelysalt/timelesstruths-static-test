Dear Reader

Our Lord is good to all those that trust in Him. We have found this to be true over and over again, and we pray that through these pages others would come to know His faithful care. There is a way to live in “heavenly places in Christ Jesus.”* (Ephesians 2:6)

We appreciate hearing from you, so please do write. We welcome questions and would be very happy to hear how the Lord has been helping you. For older levels that want to be challenged and encouraged in Christian living, Foundation Truth is also available for youths and adults.

We are a God-fearing family that includes Rick and Krista Erickson, Laura (21), Skye (20), Joel (20), Kara (17), Erik (16), and Amanda (9). The publishing of Treasures of the Kingdom is mainly done by Laura and Joel, with help (guidance, proofreading, and contributions) from others. We appreciate your prayers, for we certainly can’t do this without God’s help!

In the King’s Service,
The Editors
