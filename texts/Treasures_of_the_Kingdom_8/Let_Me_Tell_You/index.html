Let Me Tell You…​

how God Helped Me

I have a praise that I want to share with you. I didn’t like listening to devotions, then I asked Jesus to help me. Now I can pay better attention during devotions, and can answer the questions afterwards.

—Age 6, WA, United States

Don’t you like to tell things? The best things to tell are the good and true things that can help others. If Jesus has helped you, if you have learned something, if you are thankful, share it with the rest of us! Jesus wants to help us and take care of us. Take all your troubles and fears to Him, and He will always be there to listen and help you. Then you can tell how good He is to you.
