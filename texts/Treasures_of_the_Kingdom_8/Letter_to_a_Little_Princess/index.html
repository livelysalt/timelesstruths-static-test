Letter to a Little Princess

Dear Princess,

In this issue Aunt Grace wasn’t able to write you all a letter, but she hopes to next time. If you will turn back in this issue, you will find the story called “Little Corners.” It is about a girl named Georgia, and I think you will like it. Do you think she was a princess of Jesus? Read it, and then you can act out the story with your paperdolls. If you learn the song, “Jesus Bids Us Shine,” you can sing in your little “corner,” too!
