Publisher’s Abstract

In this moving account, Effie Williams traces the lives of two people as they endure hardships, find the peace of God, and enter into His service. We regret, however, that the book closes without any of the characters getting free from sectarianism and finding true unity in the church of God. Yet this is a true story, and God accepts those who will walk in the light He has given them.
