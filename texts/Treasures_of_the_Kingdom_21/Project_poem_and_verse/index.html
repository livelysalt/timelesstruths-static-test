Project, poem, and verse

Inheritance Envelope

Take: Bible, envelope, paper and pen

Search for God’s promises to you. Write them down and keep them in the envelope. You can use different colors for different kinds of promises (help, joy, safety). What is your part? Ask God to show you how to have them.

Choose Today

Oh, does it seem hard
To do what is right?
To love what is good,
To walk in the light?
The choice is yours,
Of what you will do—
Give in to the wrong,
Or stand for the true.
Remember yet
That God sees it all,
His power and grace
He’ll send when you call.
So pray for help
To conquer the wrong;
You’ll win every time
With Jesus along!
Then choose today
On which side to fight,
For me, I serve God,
Who helps me do right.

—from Joshua 24:15

A verse to hide in your heart
