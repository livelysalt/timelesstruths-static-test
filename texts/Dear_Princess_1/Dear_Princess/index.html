Dear Princess

Dear Princess is a newsletter for young girls who seek and strive to please their Father, the King. Each issue is filled with encouraging stories, poems, and articles. This is a free publication for anyone who requests it. Stories, poems, testimonies, recipes, and letters are always welcome. If the Lord has blessed you with any special thought, send it in. Share your blessing with others, that they may be blessed, too.

Dear Princess is edited by Abigail Spinks. I love to receive long, encouraging letters. Please feel free to write about any question you may have. We may not be able to print all of them, but we will try. Please tell me if you do not want your letter printed.

Your sister in Christ,
Abigail Grace
