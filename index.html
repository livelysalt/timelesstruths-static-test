Welcome

Timeless Truths is a free online library with resources including books, midi, sheet music, and more. It is dedicated to the edification of those who love God, and to seriously address the questions and concerns of those who wish to inquire about the Bible and how we should live. “Blessed are they which do hunger and thirst after righteousness: for they shall be filled.”*

Disclaimer

Inclusion of a work in our online library does not mean that we fully agree with the contents. In order to provide as much good literature as possible, we have included works which, though containing wholesome truths, also contain some error. For such works we will usually include a Publisher’s Abstract. And remember that the standard to judge a book by is the Book from God Himself.
