Privacy Policy

You may not have known it, but every move you make on our website is being watched.

Now that I’ve been blunt about it, let me try to put you more at ease. In the first case, the information that we gather does not include your credit card information or even your name. Like many sites, we get information on which pages you look at, the time you look at them, and your IP address. If you are uncomfortable with this happening, we suggest that you leave, or get software to browse the web anonymously.

The reason this information is gathered is to, hopefully, be able to make this site better, by seeing the general trends of what our users are doing.
