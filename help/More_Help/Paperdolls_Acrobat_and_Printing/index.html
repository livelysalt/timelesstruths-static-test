Paperdolls—Acrobat and Printing

In nearly every issue of Treasures of the Kingdom we feature a paperdoll item to go with “Little Princess.”

To print out the paperdoll, you will need the free Acrobat Reader, which you probably already have. With the reader installed, you can download the paperdolls and accessories, such as Princess Precious and gown. They will open up in the browser window with the reader plugin, and you can print them out or save them. When printing, be sure to set the scale to 100%, so they will turn out the right size.
