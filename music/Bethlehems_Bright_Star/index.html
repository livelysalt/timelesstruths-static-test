play midi
  1. The Magi of the East,
    All coming from afar,
    Were guided by the friendly light
    Of Bethlehem’s bright star.
    • Refrain:
      O Star, bright, beautiful Star,
      Shine on us from the blue,
      And guide our little pilgrim feet
      Our risen Lord to view.
  2. The Magi of the East
    Enjoyed the happy lot
    Of following that brilliant guide
    Which marked the sacred spot.
  3. May we, the favored few,
    Be guided from above,
    That we may follow on to know
    The bliss of perfect love.
  4. The Magi of the East
    Extolled the Infant King;
    But we “behold the Lamb of God”—
    To Him our carols sing.
