  1. Thy Word is like a garden, Lord, with flowers bright and fair;
    And everyone who seeks may pluck a lovely cluster there.
    Thy Word is like a deep, deep mine; and jewels rich and rare
    Are hidden in its mighty depths for every searcher there.
  2. Thy Word is like a starry host: a thousand rays of light
    Are seen to guide the traveler and make his pathway bright.
    Thy Word is like an armory, where soldiers may repair;
    And find, for life’s long battle day, all needful weapons there.
  3. Oh, may I love Thy precious Word, may I explore the mine,
    May I its fragrant flowers glean, may light upon me shine!
    Oh, may I find my armor there! Thy Word my trusty sword,
    I’ll learn to fight with every foe the battle of the Lord.
