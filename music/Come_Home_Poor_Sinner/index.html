Scripture: Matthew 11:28

Come Home, Poor Sinner

H. R. Jeffrey, 1885

Copyright: Public Domain

Key: F

Meter: 5.4.5.4.5.5.5.4.5.4

view score sheet music

play midi
  1. Come home, poor sinner;
    Why longer roam,
    Thy Savior’s calling,
    “Come home, come home!”
    • Refrain:
      Jesus is pleading;
      He’s interceding;
      Yes, pleading, pleading
      For thee to come;
      Come home, poor sinner,
      Come home, come home.
  2. He died to save you
    On Calvary;
    Behold what suff’ring!
    ’Twas all for thee.
  3. Oh, come to Jesus,
    Do not delay;
    Come, and He’ll save you;
    Come while you may.
  4. Oh, come to Jesus;
    He’s waiting still
    With His salvation,
    Thy soul to fill.
  5. Oh, come to Jesus;
    How can you stay,
    He’s pleading, pleading;
    Come, come today.
