Scripture: Psalm 91:2

The Pilgrim's Confidence

Barney E. Warren

Copyright: Public Domain

Key: D♭

Meter: 9.8.9.8.9.8.9.8.9.8

view score sheet music

play midi
  1. Walking through life’s dark, shady valley,
    Walking with Jesus day by day;
    Crossing the vale through death’s cold alley,
    He will be with me all the way.
    Keep me, O Lord, from harm and danger,
    Trusting Thy grace while here I stay;
    I am a pilgrim and a stranger,
    Help me to humbly watch and pray;
    I am a pilgrim and a stranger,
    Help me to humbly watch and pray.
  2. If the sun shine my heart is cheerful,
    If the clouds come I can rejoice;
    Help me, O Lord, to ever be thankful;
    I have, like Mary, made the wise choice.
    Precious Redeemer, keep me faithful,
    By Thy own strength and grace I stand;
    Help me do right and live more grateful,
    Soon I shall reach the shining strand;
    Help me do right and live more grateful,
    Soon I shall reach the shining strand.
  3. Welcome the storms, my hope is abounding;
    Let the waves come, my anchor is sure;
    Fixed in the Rock on which I am standing,
    How can I fall when all is secure?
    Wonderful peace in Thee I’m possessing,
    Vict’ry through Christ I ever shall sing;
    Let the rain fall in showers of blessing,
    Homage and praise to Thee I would bring;
    Let the rain fall in showers of blessing,
    Homage and praise to Thee I would bring.
