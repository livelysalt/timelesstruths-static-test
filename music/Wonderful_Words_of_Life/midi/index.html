  1. Sing them over again to me,

    Wonderful words of life;

    Wonderful words of life;

    Wooing us to heaven.

    Wonderful words of life;
    Offer pardon and peace to all,
    Wonderful words of life;
    Jesus, only Savior,
    Sanctify us forever.
