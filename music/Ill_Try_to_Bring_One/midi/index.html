  1. I have a dear Savior, the best Friend I know,
    While daily my life-race I run,
    I’ll try to help others as onward I go,
    And into His keeping bring one.
    • Refrain:
      Each soul that we save is a star in our crown,
      When heaven’s glad morning’s begun;
      If I cannot do more ere life’s sun goeth down
      I will bring to the Savior just one.
  2. So many are walking in pathways of sin,
    Who gladly the evil would shun
    If someone would help them the vict’ry to win;
    I’ll try to the Christ to bring one.
  3. So many grow weary and fall in the fight,
    Their journey to heav’n just begun;
    Perhaps they are watching my dim little light—
    I’ll try in His name to bring one.
  4. I’ll stand in the presence of Jesus my King
    Some day when my work is all done:
    With all of the ransomed I’ll joyfully sing,
    If unto my Lord I bring one.
