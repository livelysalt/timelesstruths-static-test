Jesus Is Good to Me

Daniel O. Teasley, pub.1911

Copyright: Public Domain

Key: F

Meter: 8.7.7.7.7.5.6.6.7.7.7.5

view score sheet music

play midi
  1. Jesus has been so good to me,
    No other friend so kind could be;
    Safely keeps me every day
    Free from sin and in the way;
    Ne’er can I such love repay,
    He’s so good to me.
    • Refrain:
      Jesus is good to me,
      Jesus is good to me;
      Safely keeps me from the wrong,
      In the conflict makes me strong,
      Blesses all my journey long,
      He’s so good to me.
  2. Jesus has been so good to me,
    From this world He set me free;
    Grace and glory did impart,
    Satisfied my longing heart:
    From His love I’ll never part,
    He’s so good to me.
  3. Jesus has been so good to me,
    And His face I soon shall see;
    Though I once afar did rove,
    Lost, unworthy of His love,
    He prepared a home above,
    He’s so good to me.
  4. Jesus has been so good to me,
    None so fair, so dear as He:
    Hallelujah! He is mine,
    Keeping me by grace divine,
    And His glories in me shine,
    He’s so good to me.
