When Jesus Answers My Prayer

Otto F. Linn, 1923

Main subject: Prayer

Scripture: Psalm 34:6

When Jesus Answers My Prayer

Andrew L. Byers, 1923

Copyright: Uncertain

Key: G

Meter: 12.9.12.7.7.7.11.7

view score sheet music

play midi
  1. Though the night has been long and my trials severe,
    And heavy my burdens of care;
    As the mists of the morning they all disappear
    When Jesus answers my prayer.
    • Refrain:
      When Jesus answers my prayer,
      When Jesus answers my prayer;
      Oh, the rapture that thrills, the glory that fills,
      When Jesus answers my prayer!
  2. Then my faith that is tried by the anguish and pain
    Of afflictions so heavy to bear
    Is rewarded at last by a wonderful gain
    When Jesus answers my prayer.
  3. Ever forward I press with my courage renewed
    And a faith that no foe can impair;
    With self and a world of allurement subdued,
    When Jesus answers my prayer.
  4. Oh, the vict’ry is won, and the glory divine,
    More precious that jewels most rare,
    Brings a lingering glow o’er this spirit of mine
    When Jesus answers my prayer.
