About

Purpose

Timeless Truths Publications was founded with the purpose of spreading the timeless truths revealed in God’s Word. We publish literature that encourages folks in walking the straight and narrow road that leads to heaven. The Timeless Truths Free Online Library is the result of our efforts to make these works as widely available as possible via the internet.

Our chief corner-stone is Jesus, and with our focus on Him, we try to avoid putting too much attention on any person or persons. If you are interested in knowing more about us and what we believe, read some issues of Foundation Truth, and feel free to contact us.

Publications

We publish two of the periodicals in print:

Foundation Truth is a magazine for youths and adults, published about twice a year, up to 36 pages.
Treasures of the Kingdom is a bi-monthly, 16-page magazine for children.
